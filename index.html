Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Innovation

Innovation drives progress by fostering creativity, advancing technology, and encouraging groundbreaking solutions to address challenges and unlock new opportunities for sustainable growth.

Sustainability

Sustainability emphasizes balancing development with environmental preservation, ensuring responsible use of resources to meet present needs without compromising the future.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure refers to the modernization and expansion of the country's physical and digital systems to drive economic growth, enhance connectivity, and improve public services for a more sustainable future.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of enabling individuals or groups to gain control, make decisions, and realize their full potential.
